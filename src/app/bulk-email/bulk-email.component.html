Bulk E-mail software

The ATSI has a bulk e-mail software module which enables the management to send across mass emails as and when required. The bulk email software comes into use when the management needs to pass on necessary information to the students or the parents. If the school organizes any urgent meetings or there is an event or in case of any important news, the school needs to inform all the parents and students and sending this information over the bulk e-mail software makes it easier for the administrative department. Through the bulk e-mail software, the administrative team can also keep track of all the received/bounced or undelivered mails. The bulk e-mail software is an extremely useful tool since the school or institute needs to pass on information to hundreds of people and sending mass mailers is the most feasible option.
