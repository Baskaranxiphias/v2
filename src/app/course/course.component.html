Syllabus Module

Syllabus module allows to define various courses that is offered in the Universities/Colleges/Schools.

Syllabus module allows to define various courses that is offered in the Universities/Colleges/Schools. While defining the syllabus, institutions can also define course types i.e., year/semester along with its duration. A.T.S.I. software allows you to define class wise subjects as per the rules and regulations (Curriculum) of the respective University boards/School boards. You can also manage class wise list of compulsory and optional subjects.

This feature allows you to:

Define the various courses offered in your school.

Define course types and duration of course.

Define grade system based on the courses being offered.

Manage different classes.

Define Compulsory and optional subjects for a specific class/ year.

Define lab sessions if any.

Assign Students to a course.

Course Management System

The syllabus management system has been designed for the teachers and the administration to manage course information over an application. It aims to help the teachers to create new proposals, prepare the syllabus and define the course structures. The course management system also keeps a track of the progress of proposals and their implementation until they are approved. It also records the results and generates charts and data to analyze performance on a monthly or yearly basis. The primary motive of the course management system is to enhance the experience of the teachers and academic staff in analyzing performance, discussing the syllabus and structuring the teaching modules.

Syllabus Registration System

The syllabus registration system is used by teachers and academic staff in colleges to design and formulate the course structure for a semester or an entire year. It successfully generates the combinations of classes and programs for a more structured approach. The course registration system also helps in recording performance over the year or semester and stores data which can be viewed in the form of charts or graphs. It is a beneficial tool for the academic staff to generate multiple schedules, register courses and view the results over a period of time.

Online Syllabus Registration System

The online syllabus registration system allows the professors and teachers to generate syllabus online and maintain records of the same. It helps in creating a one-stop database for all the classes and program for the ease of access and usage. Most schools and colleges offer various combinations of subjects and since a large number of students opt for these courses, the online system makes it easier for the teachers as well as the students to plan their schedule. The online course registration system aims to make the syllabus defining process more planned and orderly.

Syllabus Management System

The syllabus management system has been designed to create a platform for syllabus management and organization. It gives the academic staff the options to define and schedule classes and subject modules in a more structured format that helps the students to choose a variety of course combinations. Also, it records class progress for every module and generates chart or graph based analysis. The stored data is used to monitor the various modules and their popularity over the years which also helps the school and college allocate time more wisely. The syllabus management system makes it easier for schools and colleges to better manage their classroom schedules and course offerings.
