Student Hostel Management Software - This Online Hostel Management Software has been designed to automate and effectively manage the entire process of gaining admission to the hostel. It gathers student information from its registration process and checks for room availability. The student is required to input all information for the process to give a super-fast output. Then it allocates rooms to the registered students and also records their fee and other advance payment details. This helps in keeping an organized record of all students using the hostel facility through the hostel management software. All information stored in this best hostel management software is accessible to the administrative department as well as the students. It also gathers a feedback on the room, food and other amenities on a regular basis and sends fee reminders to all registered students and has proved to be an effective method of managing student information in the hostel premises.
